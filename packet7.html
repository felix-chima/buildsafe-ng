Campaign coherence — one argument across every platform

Every format makes the same core claim: compliance is not a document, it is verifiable proof. The same shield logo, navy-and-green palette, Space Grotesk headlines and “Trust, Verified.” sign-off carry from the newspaper page to the radio chime, the TV end card, the billboard and the feed. The line “Approved. Built. Proven.” anchors the TV, billboard and print work, while the proof points (₦0 demolition orders, 99.2% compliance, geotagged inspections) repeat across social — so a viewer who hears the radio spot, then sees the billboard, then meets a post, receives one consistent message reinforced from four directions. That repetition is what builds brand recall and trust before the sales call.
